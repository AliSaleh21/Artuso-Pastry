Welcome to our Artuso Pastry

Rooted in generations of family tradition, Artuso Pastry isn’t just a bakery—it’s a legacy of love, community, and craftsmanship. As you walk through our doors or browse our offerings online, you’re not just selecting a dessert; you’re joining a story that began nearly a century ago in Reggio di Calabria, Italy, and continues to thrive in the heart of the Bronx. Our commitment goes beyond flavor—we believe in using only the finest ingredients, time-tested techniques, and a personal touch that makes every customer feel like family. From seasonal specialties to timeless classics, we strive to deliver moments of joy, one pastry at a time. Discover why Artuso Pastry is cherished across generations, and let us be part of your sweetest memories.

We wish you eternity of happiness !
